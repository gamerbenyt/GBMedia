Get My Cash - Kal Nova

Publishing

One of the first songs made by Kal Nova that was published to platforms, and one of the first songs published by GB Media. Because of this, we have a special place in our hearts for it.

Drift (feat. Gamer Ben) - Kal Nova

Publishing, Mastering

Drift started as a draft from November 12, 2025, until a meeting with GB Media where, when looking for drafts of songs to finish and publish, we found it. After listening to it, Kal Nova enjoyed the song, even though there were only about 4 bars done, enough to want to finish it. The chorus is the only part currently that contains the old vocals, and this is why during Gamer Ben's modification to the chorus, it still has some of Kal Nova's vocals.

KAIRO – Lost in Echoes

Production, Sound Design, Mastering

This one began as a late-night atmosphere: glassy synths, heavy space, and a voice almost swallowed by reverb. GB Media turned that mood into a cinematic track with detail in the echoes and weight in the master.

ZAYNIX – Pressure

Beat Production, Mixing

Pressure was written to feel restless. The beat was pushed toward a darker street pulse, then mixed with clipped energy and red-light tension so the track keeps moving even when the vocal gets cold and minimal.

AYA V – Glitter Tears

Production, Vocal Tuning, Mastering

AYA V wanted polish without losing the ache. GB Media tuned the vocal transparently, widened the shimmer around the chorus, and mastered the final record so the glossy surface still carries the hurt underneath.

JAXON REE – No Signal

Production, Mixing, Mastering

No Signal came from distortion, isolation, and a hook that felt like it was breaking through static. GB Media leaned into the glitch, cleaned the low end, and made the final track hit like a transmission finally locking in.
